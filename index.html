Making of 'Modern Vintage'

Mark Burkett / Vimeo
This micro documentary gives the audience an insight into the making of 'Modern Vintage', the new album from CHEAP FAKES. With renowned producer - John Merchant (Michael Jackson, Lenny Kravitz, Bee Gees, Barbara Streisand and many more) on board, the band wanted to give people a look at the recording process, in what has been Cheap Fakes biggest undertaking to date.
